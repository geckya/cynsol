At Soliz Law, we believe in the power of individualized attention. This firm was started by Cynthia Soliz to offer an alternative to the personal injury firms in Austin. By taking fewer cases, we are able to actively engage with our clients, get to know their stories, be able to explain their challenges better, and maximize their recovery. All our current clients have their attorney's personal cell phone number and can call them with questions.

Sometimes, all you need to know is how to navigate a basic claim yourself.

Cynthia Soliz is a proud graduate of Texas A&M (Class of '04) and The University of Texas School of Law (2009). For years she worked for one of the largest personal injury firms in the state and handled a high-volume caseload. She has been exclusively practicing liability law in Austin, TX for over six years.
